Concierge Services

Boating License Assistance

Don't have a boating license? No problem! We provide assistance in obtaining a temporary boating license so you can legally operate our boats. Our team will guide you through the process and ensure you have all necessary documentation.

Driver Option

If you prefer not to get a license, we offer a driver service for $25/hr who will operate the boat while you enjoy the scenery. Our professional drivers are experienced and knowledgeable about the local lakes.
