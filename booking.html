Frequently Asked Questions

What if I don't have a boating license?

No problem! We offer two solutions: we can assist you in obtaining a temporary boating license, or you can hire one of our drivers for $25/hour.

Is delivery included?

We offer free delivery to Heffley Lake, Paul Lake, and Kamloops Lake. For other locations, there's a delivery fee of $50 per 100 km from Kamloops.

What's included in the rental?

All rentals include the inflatable boat, life jackets, and paddles. You can add extras like coolers, speakers, and dry bags for an additional fee.

What's your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 48 hours or more before your scheduled rental receive a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours will receive a 50% refund.
